Window

Aspire Infra offers a range of UPVC windows for home and commercial setups. Our products do not just overhaul the appearance of your home, but also protect your premises from external elements like dust, noise, pollution and rain. We are observant of the changing trends and the preferences of our clients and do not stick to traditional windows. You can try out our bay, sliding or casement windows. Select any of our available designs and tailor them as per the interiors of your residential, commercial or industrial establishment. Browse through our repertoire of UPVC windows and make a choice with a difference.

Sliding Windows


Perfect for areas with limited space, our sliding windows open sideways with a slight push. Ideal for modern establishments, they fetch finesse to your room. Get adequate outside view, ventilation and optimum natural light in your premises, whether commercial or domestic.

Casement Windows


Add style and elegance to your property with our casement windows. They can open both outward and inward, thanks to the hinges. Such windows are made from mullions, sashes and small, strong frames.

Bay Windows


Our window experts create the perfect little corner in your office or bedroom with windows that protrude from the main walls, making way for a bay inside the room. They are a combination of sliders, casement and fixed windows, positioned at various angles from each other.

Combination Windows


Integrate an attractive and strong frame with customized design and shapes. A mix of casement, sliding and fixed windows or two windows of the same type, combination windows are long-lasting and are ideal for thermal insulation.

Windows with Louvers


Let 100% air flow in through opened louvre windows. When closed, these windows keep the wind and water out. They are aesthetically appealing, durable, suit all architectural styles and are a perfect blend of the traditional and contemporary.
